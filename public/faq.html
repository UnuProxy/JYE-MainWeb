Frequently Asked Questions

  • How much does it cost to hire a boat in Ibiza Town?
    The cost varies widely depending on the yacht's size, level of luxury, and charter duration. Day charters can start from a few thousand euros, while a week-long hire on a superyacht may cost tens of thousands.
  • What's the best time to experience Ibiza by yacht?
    For the liveliest atmosphere, July and August are peak season. If you prefer a quieter pace, May, June, September, and October are lovely, with the added bonus of fewer crowds.
  • What are the must-visit spots on a yacht in Ibiza and Formentera?

  • What is included in a yacht charter fee?
    Our yacht charter fee typically includes the rental of the yacht, the attendance of professional staff, and all necessary safety equipment. Additional costs such as fuel, marina fees, food and beverages, and special requests can be arranged for an extra fee. It's important to discuss these details upfront to tailor the charter to your preferences while managing budget expectations.
  • What is the cancellation policy for a boat rental in Ibiza?
    • More than 90 days before the charter start date: Full refund, less an administrative fee.
    • 60 to 89 days before the charter start date: 50% refund of the total charter fee.
    • Less than 60 days: No refunds available.
    We recommend travel insurance to cover unforeseen circumstances such as illness or travel disruptions.
  • What should I do if I have a complaint?
    Immediate Concerns: Inform the captain or head crew member as soon as possible to resolve issues during your charter.
    Formal Complaints: Submit a written complaint within 14 days after the charter's conclusion to info@justenjoyibiza.com. We aim to resolve all complaints within 30 days of receipt.
