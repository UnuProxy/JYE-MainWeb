About Us

Since 2016
Unmatched Experience in Ibiza

Welcome to Just Enjoy Ibiza, where luxury meets personalised service. With years of experience since 2016, we strive to offer unforgettable moments on the beautiful waters of Ibiza. We take great pride in providing a bespoke yachting experience that exceeds expectations, backed by our passion for the sea and our deep knowledge of the industry.

Myself, Julian, along with my trusted associate, Alin, have built a reputation for delivering excellence with every charter. Our goal is to make your time aboard our yachts seamless, luxurious, and truly memorable—whether you are looking to explore hidden coves, enjoy water sports, or simply relax and take in the breathtaking views.
